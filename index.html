Health is Wealth, Know your BMI today!

A BMI (Body Mass Index) calculator is a tool that uses a person's weight and height to estimate their body fat percentage, which can help determine if they are within a healthy weight range. It's a common method used by healthcare providers to assess an individual's health and risk factors.

BMI Classification

BMI < 18.5 → Underweight

18.5 – 24.9 → Normal weight

25 – 29.9 → Overweight

30 and above → Obese

Health Implications

Underweight: May lead to nutrient deficiencies and low immunity.

Overweight: Higher risk of heart disease, diabetes, and joint issues.

Obese: Significantly increases health risks including hypertension and stroke.

Disclaimer

This tool is a general guide. It may not be accurate for athletes, pregnant women, or children.
